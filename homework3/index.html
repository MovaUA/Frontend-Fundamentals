Roasted Beet Salad

Roasted beets with balsamic vinegar dressing.

It is my favorite salad because I love learning javascript!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium beets, trimmed and scrubbed
  • 2 tablespoons aged balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons real maple syrup
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). ♨️
  2. Wrap beets loosely in aluminum foil and place on a rimmed baking sheet. 🧻
  3. Roast in the prheated oven until easily pierced with a knife or skewer, 50 to 60 minutes. ⏱️
  4. Unwrap and cool until easily handled, about 10 minutes. 🆒
  5. Peel beets and cut into chunks. 🍌
  6. Mix vinegar and maple syrup together; season with salt and pepper. our over beets. 🎨
  7. Refrigerate until beets absorb the flavors, at least 1 hour. 🥶
  8. Serve cold. 🥗
